## Numbers Solver
![](http://datagenetics.com/blog/august42014/title.png)


This web page solves Countdown game show numbers games. Click here for [more background information](http://datagenetics.com/blog/august32014/index.html).

## Enter the numbers

Enter the number tiles in the six boxes below. There can be no more than two of any number in the range *1-10*, and optionally one each of the numbers *25, 50, 75, 100*. Enter the desired target solution in the lower box, then click Solve!
