Proxi is hiring a Data Scientist to join our growing data team!

Proxi is a game where you grow an Artificial Intelligence based on your memories. Designed by Will Wright; creator of The Sims, Spore and SimCity; this mobile (product) is set to be released in late 2019. https://www.proxiai.com/

We’re looking for people to join our team. The team enjoys daily lunches together, is highly creative, and has a penchant for playing with magnets. We work out of a quirky loft in West Berkeley. Perks include Wednesday team lunches, dog friendly office, flexible work and vacation schedules.

What you’ll do:

- Help us improve models supporting a quirky personalized AI

- Support these models across multiple languages and cultures

- Explore Player interaction providing insight to improve players experience

- Anomaly detection


Desired Experience:

- BS/MS degree in Computer Science, Engineering, related degree, or relevant professional experience

- 2-5 years’ professional experience

- Pragmatic approach to problem solving that integrates both short term and long term goals

- Experience implementing models in production


Bonus Experience:

- Background in natural language understanding (NLP/NLU)

- Multilingual

- Passionate about protecting players information

- Interest in Neural, Cognitive and Behavioral Science
