**The Opportunity**

The world-class Data Science and AI Group within the Faculty of Information Technology is expanding further, and looking for an enthusiastic Lecturer or Senior Lecturer (equivalent to Assistant and Associate Professors in the US).

We currently are seeking a Lecturer/Senior Lecturer for our IT for Energy theme. As Monash’s energy research front, Monash Energy Materials and Systems Institute (MEMSI) is focused on developing solutions to enable sustainable generation, storage, distribution, visualisation and end-use of energy. This balance between multi-faculty expertise, academic excellence and industry application enables MEMSI to work effectively with industry partners to address significant energy challenges.

For more information about the exciting initiatives being conducted in IT for Energy, please visit: https://www.monash.edu/memsi, and our Microgrid based living laboratory that is part of our Net Zero Initiative (https://www.monash.edu/net-zero-initiative)

You will be responsible for:

Conducting high quality research
Attracting research funding
Contributing to the teaching effort of the IT for Energy theme
Mentoring undergraduate and postgraduate students

You will be innovative and collaborative, with a doctoral qualification, with excellent research publications record, and demonstrated teaching experience.

This role is a full-time position; however, flexible working arrangements may be negotiated.
