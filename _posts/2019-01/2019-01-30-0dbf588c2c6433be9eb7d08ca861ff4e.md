## What Dataiku Brings to the Table

From the very beginning here at Dataiku, we’ve always fostered the idea of data democratization. But it’s not just talk - the Dataiku team has been working hard (though our product offering, but also through devotion to data meetup communities, non-profit and education advancement, and more) to ensure that data teams and departments around the world can succeed:

1. Dataiku provides one simple UI for data wrangling, mining, visualization, machine learning, and deployment based on a collaborative and team-based user interface **accessible to anyone on a data team**, from data scientist to beginner analyst.

1. Dataiku delivers a **shortcut to machine learning and AI**deployment/management, making data science more accessible and intuitive for the less experienced (and more streamlined, stable, and repeatable for those who still prefer to code).

1. Dataiku is the scalable path to operationalization, allowing enterprises to **go from raw data to prototyping/sandbox to production easily**.
