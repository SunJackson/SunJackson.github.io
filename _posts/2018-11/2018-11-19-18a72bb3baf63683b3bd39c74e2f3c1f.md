**Position Description**

The **Director, Omni-channel Analytics** position is a client-facing role requiring both technical analytics and direct-to-consumer marketing business knowledge and skills. This role supports the efforts to craft the overall vision for how leveraging web/digital data integrates with other channels to support the multi-channel analytics and consumer efforts. Working across business, IT, and analytic teams, gains an in-depth knowledge and understanding of the key consumer touch points across channels, technology platforms, and available solutions to generate real-time insights and communications. Leverage hands-on technical and digital marketing business acumen to drive reporting, analytic insights, and modeling. 

**Please note: **This position is based in our Minnetonka, MN office and is not a work from home position.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

Drive useful insights that will connect the consumer experience across the business
Collaborate effectively with key business lines, technology partners, vendors and architects to drive toward multi-channel analytic capabilities driving business decisions
Represent the consumer in the UHC experience across the different channels
Lead and/or collaborate with other analytics professionals across the organization to measurably improve the consumer experience

**Required Qualifications:**

Bachelor degree in Mathematics, Statistics or similar field
2+ years of experience utilizing analytics tools and databases, including SAS, R and other databases
2+ years of experience with advanced analytics, including machine learning and artificial intelligence
2+ years of experience as a database analyst, specifically with large databases 
Web analytics and reporting leveraging Adobe Analytics or similar tools
Managing and building digital analytics data transformation (importing/exporting) to combine online/offline channel interactions reporting and analysis
Understanding of traffic-driving mechanisms to an online website and interpreting key website behavioral variables
Demonstrated knowledge of web design structure and tagging for digital analytics data capture
Demonstrated user skills in MS Access, Excel and Word
Demonstrated communication skills

**Preferred Qualification:**

Master’s Degree
Experience utilizing Oracle and DB2 Database tool
Demonstrated ability to effectively prioritize and manage the needs of multiple stakeholders
Demonstrated ability to communicate technical or detailed information effectively to nontechnical audiences
Demonstrated marketing domain knowledge
Project management skills
Experience delivering direct-to-consumer analytics and solutions oriented to real-time communication
Business Intelligence tool implementation and management, such as Tableau
Experience working with statistics to perform analysis, data modeling, and predictive behavioral modeling
Knowledge of Power Point
Healthcare industry experience
