**Position Description**

The Digital Analytics team is looking for an experienced leader responsible for implementing the Business Intelligence Data strategy providing scale to our reporting and analytic teams by maintaining the team's big data tenant.

As the **Manager, Data Engineering and Architecture**, you’ll work with our report developers and analyst to help set the vision and deliver data assets that drive insights and opportunities for the digital product teams. Your team will source and structure clickstream data feeds and call center data with offline data to drive scale across the lines of business.  Most importantly, you’ll play a key leadership role in shaping the analytics culture across the growing team.

The ideal candidate will have experience working in modern data architectures designing and implementing data marts that enable analytics and semantic layers that drive BI tools.  We’re looking for someone who has the technical skills to quickly navigate a complex environment and develop strategic views of the business that communicate insights in a way that drives action.

Please note - this position is office based in Minnetonka, MN and is not open to work from home/telecommute.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

Set and deliver the data strategy that supports reporting and analytics efforts to increase the maturity of the BI team
Create blueprints for big data tenant to integrate, centralize, protect and maintain data sources
Develops, constructs, tests, and maintains architectures
Sources data via methods such as http GET and JSON APIs
Oversee the ETL of data across staging areas
Conduct code reviews of teams’ Spark Streaming programs in Scala, Java, Python, and R
Recommend ways to improve data reliability, efficiency, and quality

**Required Qualifications:**

8+ years of experience in data engineering and/or data science
2+ years people management experience
Demonstrated track record of implementing BI strategy and underlying data architecture
Experience building cross-functional relationships and communicating at all levels of an organization
Experience using Big Data Processing Technologies such as Hadoop, Spark, Hive, and/or Pig
Knowledgeable of complex SQL queries and ETL
Demonstrated understanding of different data formats and best practices for analytic practitioners and reporting applications

**Preferred Qualifications:**

Inquisitive with a passion for data architecture design patterns
Demonstrated analyst mindset
Experience working in a large, matrixed organization
Attention to detail and ability to produce high caliber work products
Ability to work in a dynamic, challenging environment where decisions you make will have a large impact
Strategic problem solver who simplifies problems to their core elements and finds creative solutions
Understand the differences between Adobe Analytics clickstream options: API, Live Stream, and Data Feeds
Experience working with Adobe clickstream data and the filters needed to match Adobe Analytics
